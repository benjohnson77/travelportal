Snow Shoe Tour

Walk through a seemingly endless white desert, across frozen lakes, snow-covered plains and hills. The only thing breaking the silence is the sound of crushing snow under your snow shoes.

Join our experienced guide for few hours of snow fun and incredible views over the snow-covered hills. Take a breather and enjoy a cup of hot tea while listening to the sound of true silence.

Snow shoe tours are organised at short notice and can easily be included on your "list of things to do" whilst visiting Nordkyn.

Snow shoes are also available for short-term or long-term rental.
